Debris Field

Website

Fight your way through an unforgiving arena, learning how to use Newtonian movement to give yourself an edge over your enemies. Upgrade your ship and your weapons along the way with random stacking upgrades, but don't take too long, as the enemy will soon overwhelm you.

Debris Field is under active development, and will continue to be updated with new content and mechanics over time. By purchasing now, you are helping to support Debris Field's Development, as well as helping to shape the game with your feedback.

Debris Field is available for download on Itch.IO, and will be available on Steam soon. There is also a playable demo available from the Itch.IO page.

Debris Field is a top down physics based space brawler with roguelike elements

Undine

Undine is a relaxing exploration game in which you navigate a gondola through the waterways of Venice.

I am using the project as an opportunity to experiment with procedural generation and exploration based gameplay mechanics, and don't really intend to create a fully fleshed out game out of it, however I will be releaseing it for free once it is in some kind of playable state.

Xonotic

Website

Xonotic is an open source quake-like online shooter, build using a fork of the id Tech 3 engine. It is still under active development.

My contribution to the project consisted of 3D artwork for weapons and vehicles, and maps which were built in the NetRadiant BSP editor. Many of the weapons and maps I created are included as official content in the current release.
