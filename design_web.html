Green Mountain Horse and Tack

Website

This site was build using the BigCommerce Stencil theming system as a base. I heavily modified the original design into something with a unique look and functionality that was more in line with the feel of the local buisiness it represented.

Standout features of the site include a slide-out tray for the cart and quick search, as well as a custom shop-by price slider widget on product pages.
